Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 15, 2026. This site is operated as an informational tool with display advertising.

Overview

LabAgenticFlow (labagenticflow.com) provides LLM API cost comparison tools. We do not require user accounts. The calculator runs in your browser; workload inputs are not stored on our servers.

Information we collect

  • Usage data — Standard web server logs (IP address, browser type, pages visited, timestamps) may be collected by our hosting provider for security and operations.
  • Analytics — We may use privacy-friendly analytics (e.g. Cloudflare Web Analytics or Plausible) to understand traffic patterns. No sale of personal data.
  • Calculator inputs — Token counts and slider values stay on your device unless you share a URL or screenshot yourself.

Advertising & cookies

We display third-party advertisements (e.g. Adsterra or similar CPM networks) to fund hosting. Ad partners may set cookies or use device identifiers to deliver and measure ads, subject to their own policies. You can control cookies through your browser settings.

We do not enable pop-under, push notification, or other intrusive ad formats on this site.

Third-party services

  • OpenRouter — Public pricing API used to build our model dataset (OpenRouter privacy).
  • Google Fonts — Typography served from Google; may log IP per Google's policy.
  • Hosting / CDN — Static files served via our deployment provider (e.g. Vercel or Cloudflare Pages).

Data retention

Server logs are retained only as long as needed for operations and security, per hosting provider defaults.

Your rights

Depending on your jurisdiction (GDPR, CCPA, etc.), you may have rights to access, correct, or delete personal data. Contact us at privacy@labagenticflow.com for requests.
